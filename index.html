Abstract

TalkLipPlus is a new SoTA method that enhances Talking Face Generation based on the TalkLip framework. It introduces improved post-processing, better data pre-processing, and refined face integration techniques. Our model significantly improves video generation quality and outperforms previous methods.

By leveraging advanced face integration methods, TalkLipPlus maintains facial consistency and enhances the realism of lip-synchronized talking face videos. The model achieves state-of-the-art performance, surpassing TalkLip and other approaches before August 2023. Training can be done with as little as 5 minutes of video, and inference supports unlimited audio input for high-fidelity video synthesis.
